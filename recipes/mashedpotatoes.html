Garlic Mashed Potatoes

Description

These garlic mashed potatoes are rich and very tasty! For a creamier texture, use heavy cream in place of the milk. These are also terrific topped with sesame seeds or green onions.

This recipe will take about 10 minutes of prep time and 15 minutes of cooking time.

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Milk and butter
  • Garlic
  • Seasonings

Steps

  1. Dice Garlic
  2. Wash and Peel Potatoes
  3. Boil Potatoes with Garlic
  4. Mash or Beat Potatoes
